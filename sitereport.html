This website is created as a part of the web assignment project given by Nami College. The purpose of the website is to share the author's knowledge and experience as a white hat hacker with others and raise awareness about ethical hacking. The website has five main sections: Home, Portfolio, Contact, Work-Experience, and Site Report.

The website has a professional and clean design. The logo used on the site is created using resources from icons8.com, which adds to the overall aesthetic of the site. The social media icons used on the site are from boxicons.com, and they are well-designed and eye-catching. The layout of the website is user-friendly and easy to navigate.

The Home section provides an introduction to the website and gives an overview of what visitors can expect to find on the site. The section is concise and to the point, making it easy for visitors to understand the purpose of the website.

The Portfolio section showcases the author's work as a white hat hacker. The section is well-organized and provides detailed information about each project, which adds credibility to their skills as a white hat hacker.

The Contact section provides visitors with a way to get in touch with the author. The section is simple and easy to use, with a contact form that visitors can fill out to send a message directly to the author. The author has also included their email address and social media links, making it easy for visitors to connect with them.

The Work-Experience section provides a detailed overview of the author's experience as a white hat hacker. The section is well-written and provides a chronological timeline of the author's work. The section is informative and gives visitors an insight into what it takes to become a successful white hat hacker.

The Site Report section provides an overview of the website, including its design, layout, and content. The section is well-written and provides valuable insights into the website's strengths and weaknesses. The section is helpful for anyone looking to create their website, as it highlights some of the key elements of a successful website.

In conclusion, this is a well-designed and informative website that provides valuable insights into the world of ethical hacking. The website is user-friendly and easy to navigate, with a professional and clean design. The author has done an excellent job of showcasing their work as a white hat hacker, providing visitors with detailed information about their projects. The website is a great resource for anyone interested in ethical hacking, and it is a great example of a well-designed and informative website.
